# 💊 Dawa AI — Kenya Medicine Information in Swahili

> *Dawa* (Swahili) = medicine, drug

Safe, accessible medicine information for Kenyan patients in plain Swahili. Drug interactions, dosage guidance, side effects, generic alternatives, and nearest pharmacy locator — all grounded in KEBS and KMPDB approved drug data.

## Why This Matters

Over 50% of Kenyans self-medicate. Drug package inserts are in English. Pharmacists are scarce in rural areas. The result: dangerous drug combinations, incorrect dosages, and preventable adverse reactions. Dawa AI provides safe, accessible medicine information in Swahili — grounded in KEBS-approved drugs, not hallucination.

## Features

- 🔍 Drug lookup by name (brand or generic)
- 💊 Dosage guidance by age/weight
- ⚠️ Drug interaction checker
- 🔄 Generic alternatives (affordability)
- 🏥 Kenya pharmacy and stockout checker
- 📋 NHIF coverage information

## Data Sources

- KMPDB (Kenya Medical Products & Devices Authority) — approved drug list
- KEBS drug standards
- Kenya Essential Medicines List (KEML)
- NHIF formulary
